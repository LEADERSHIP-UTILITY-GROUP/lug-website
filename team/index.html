Who we are

Two people. One problem worth solving.

Childhood friends from Montana who went in different directions — and ended up at the same table with the same conviction about what professional organizations deserve.

Caricature coming soon

James

Co-Founder

James came to LUG through the most direct possible route: watching his wife — a physician serving as treasurer of a national medical specialty society, on top of her clinical and teaching responsibilities — spend evenings and weekends on work that software should have been handling for years.

He spent two decades at some of the world's largest professional services firms before moving into the technology industry, where he built expertise in operational design, financial strategy, and building systems that actually hold up under real-world conditions. When the opportunity emerged to apply that work to professional associations, he knew exactly what needed to be built.

At LUG, James runs the back of the house — product, operations, finance, and client delivery. His measure of success is whether the work gets done before anyone has to ask for it.

William

Co-Founder

William has spent more than two decades helping small organizations and independent professionals find solutions that work in practice, not just on paper. His career was built on the kind of trust that only comes from following through — thousands of times, with thousands of clients who had real stakes and limited patience for anything that fell short.

He has a rare combination of instincts: he knows how to find the right fit between what an organization needs and what a service can actually deliver, and he knows how to earn the kind of relationship where clients call him first when something goes wrong, not last.

At LUG, William runs the front of the house — client relationships, new partnerships, and making sure every organization we work with feels like our only one. His measure of success is whether clients want to renew.

Montana, then everywhere else

One went west. One went east. Both ended up here.

James and William grew up together in Montana before life sent them in different directions — James east to Chicago and then New York and Washington, William west to build a career serving professionals and small businesses across the country.

Decades later, a physician's impossible workload brought them back to the same conversation. What they built together reflects both of their instincts: the operational discipline to deliver excellent service reliably, and the relationship intelligence to understand what excellent actually means to someone who went to medical school to practice medicine, not to manage a back office.

LUG is what you get when two people who grew up valuing hard work and straight talk decide to apply both to a market that hasn't seen enough of either.
