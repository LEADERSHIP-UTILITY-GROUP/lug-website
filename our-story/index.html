Our story

Built by people who watched the problem from the inside

LUG exists because brilliant professionals deserve to spend their time on the work that matters — not the administrative overhead that quietly consumes it.

Where it began

A physician volunteered to lead. She didn't volunteer for everything that came with it.

She is a physician — clinical responsibilities, academic teaching, a family at home. When she volunteered to serve as treasurer of a national specialty medical society, she understood it would take some of her time. What she didn't expect was how much of it.

Evenings and weekends spent reconciling expenses by hand. A website that needed to be rebuilt from scratch to update a single line of text. An email inbox that their service provider had quietly stopped monitoring. All of it paid for — handsomely — by an organization that trusted someone else to handle it.

We've been able to order a car, a meal, or a prescription from our phones for nearly two decades. The technology to make professional organizations run smoothly has existed for years. The problem was never the tools. It was who was using them — and who wasn't.

Two people who knew her well looked at this and reached the same conclusion: the professionals leading these organizations deserve better, the tools exist to deliver it, and someone should build the service to connect the two.

"She didn't go to medical school to spend her weekends doing work that technology should have automated years ago."

Our mission

Free leaders to lead. We carry everything else.

Professional organizations exist to advance their fields, support their members, and set standards of practice. That requires real leadership — and real leaders should be spending their time on that mission, not on bookkeeping, annual filings, or inbox management.

LUG takes the operational weight off the people who have more important things to do. We combine experienced service with modern tools and a purpose-built member portal — so your leadership team has full visibility into what matters, and none of the administrative burden that doesn't.

Flat monthly pricing. No surprise invoices. A team that picks up the phone. And because no two organizations are identical, we keep asking what we can build next — the first version of anything we deliver is never the last.
