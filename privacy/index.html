LUG Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 · Effective April 3, 2026 · Last Updated April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Leadership Utility Group LLC (“LUG,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is an association management company that provides financial administration, member management, and technology services to small professional societies (“client organizations”) across the United States. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all individuals who interact with our platform.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use our website at lug.io and our associated services (collectively, the “Services”). By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to the following categories of individuals:

  • Society administrators: Officers and authorized representatives of client organizations who use LUG’s platform to manage their society’s finances, membership, and operations.
  • Society members: Individual members of client organizations who interact with LUG’s platform to view their membership status, payment history, or make payments.
  • Website visitors: Individuals who visit lug.io or interact with our marketing materials.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account registration information: name, email address, phone number, and role within your organization.
  • Organization information: society name, EIN (if provided), mailing address, and officer contact details.
  • Payment information: when you make payments through our platform, payment details are collected and processed directly by Stripe. LUG does not receive, store, or have access to your full credit card or bank account numbers for payment purposes.
  • Communications: messages you send to us via email, support requests, or feedback forms.

3.2 Financial Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

To provide financial administration services, LUG connects to your organization’s bank accounts through third-party financial data aggregators. When a society administrator authorizes a bank connection, we collect the following information through these services:

  • Account information: bank name, account name, account type (checking, savings), and masked account numbers.
  • Account balances: current and available balances.
  • Transaction history: transaction dates, amounts, descriptions, categories, and merchant names.

We use the following third-party services for bank connectivity:

  • Plaid: When you connect your bank account through Plaid, you authenticate directly with your financial institution through Plaid’s secure interface (Plaid Link). LUG receives an access token that allows us to retrieve your financial data through Plaid’s API. LUG never receives your bank login credentials. Plaid’s use of your data is governed by Plaid’s End User Privacy Policy, available at https://plaid.com/legal.
  • Sophtron: Sophtron is an additional financial data aggregator we use to support bank connections. When connecting through Sophtron, you authenticate directly with your financial institution through Sophtron’s secure Universal Connect Widget. LUG never receives your bank login credentials.

3.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the platform.
  • Device and browser information: browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • IP address and approximate location derived from IP address.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: we use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide financial administration, member management, and reporting services to your organization.
  • Account management: To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and manage access to your organization’s data.
  • Financial reporting: To generate financial dashboards, transaction summaries, budgets, and reports for your organization’s administrators.
  • Payment processing: To facilitate dues collection and other payments between organizations and their members through Stripe.
  • Communications: To send you service-related notifications, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • Security and fraud prevention: To detect, prevent, and respond to security incidents, fraud, and abuse of our Services.
  • Service improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the functionality, performance, and user experience of our platform.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 With Your Organization

If you are a member of a client organization, your membership information and payment history are visible to your organization’s authorized administrators through LUG’s platform. Each organization can only access data belonging to its own members.

5.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who process data on our behalf to deliver our Services:

ProviderPurpose and Data Shared
CloudflareWebsite hosting and security. Processes HTTP request data (IP addresses, request headers) for DDoS protection and content delivery.
SupabaseDatabase hosting and authentication. Stores account information, organization data, and financial data retrieved from aggregators.
StripePayment processing. Processes payment card information and transaction data. LUG does not store card numbers.
PlaidBank data aggregation. Retrieves account information, balances, and transaction history from connected financial institutions.
SophtronBank data aggregation. Retrieves account information, balances, and transaction history from connected financial institutions.

All service providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes specified by LUG.

5.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, prevent fraud, or protect the personal safety of users or the public.

5.4 Business Transfers

If LUG is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify affected users before their personal information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel using multi-factor authentication.
  • Row-level security policies ensure that each organization’s data is isolated from other organizations.
  • We conduct periodic access reviews and security assessments.
  • We never store bank login credentials. Authentication with financial institutions occurs directly between you and your bank through our aggregator partners’ secure interfaces.

For more information about our security practices, please refer to our Information Security Policy and Access Control Policy, available upon request.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Active accounts: Your data is retained for the duration of your organization’s service relationship with LUG.
  • After termination: When an organization terminates its relationship with LUG, all associated personal data is deleted from our database within 30 days.
  • Bank connections: Aggregator connections (Plaid and Sophtron access tokens) are revoked at the time of account termination.
  • Backups: Database backups containing deleted data are automatically rotated and purged within 90 days.
  • Legal obligations: We may retain certain information for longer periods where required by law, such as for tax, accounting, or regulatory purposes.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Disconnection: You may disconnect your bank account at any time through your organization’s dashboard, which will revoke our access to your financial data going forward.
  • Opt-out of communications: You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@lug.io. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • The right to delete your personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. LUG does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at privacy@lug.io.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at privacy@lug.io.

11. Third-Party Links and Services

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by LUG. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our platform, including:

  • Plaid: https://plaid.com/legal
  • Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • Sophtron: https://sophtron.com/privacy

12. International Data Transfers

LUG is based in the United States and our Services are hosted on infrastructure located in the United States. If you access our Services from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also provide additional notice, such as sending an email to registered users. Your continued use of our Services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
